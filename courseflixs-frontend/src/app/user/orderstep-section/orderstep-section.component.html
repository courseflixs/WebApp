How To Make An Order?
1. Select your courses
Find the right course for you from DIC library
2. Add it to the shopping cart
After found the course you love, just click “add to cart”. Don’t forget if You buy 3 courses or more at the same time, you will get 30% off
3. Complete the checkout form
Full fill the form and complete checkout. You can pay with paypal or bitcoin.
4. Enter your email
Your email is important. So, pls register with your real email to make sure you will get the download links after payment
5. Receive your course & enjoy!
Done. You can check email to get the download link immediately or click to your order history in your dashboard.
